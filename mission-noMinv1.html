The American Computerized Bible website is a mission of love that the Lord has laid on my heart, and it currently contains eight different versions of the Holy Bible, it's a work in progress, and it's only one part of my ministry. These Bible versions have been assembled because Jesus said, in Mark 16:15-18 "Go out into all the world, and preach the gospel to every creature. Anyone that believes and is baptized will be saved, but whoever does not believe will be condemned." Many years of dedicated research have gone into assembling what I hope you will find to be a high quality and useful online Bible. I believe if someone out there, even if it's only one person finds salvation through this effort, my mission will have been accomplished.

The Twenty-First Century Bible is the second part of this ministry, and it's included as part of the American Computerized Bible website. The Twenty-First Century Bible is going to be modern, new, tewnty-first century version of the Bible that's being translated from various versions of ancient biblical texts, including the King James Version which was first published in 1611. The Twenty-First Century Bible is a living Biblical translation that's inspired by the teachings of Jesus Christ. It's a gift from God for people of the twenty-first century and beyond to study. As of May 6, 2023, except for editing and proof reading, translation through the book of Leviticus Chapter 14 is mostly complete, that is until God's spirit moves me tomorrow. And it's my mission from God to preach the gospel to the whole world.

God loves you, I love you, and this is God's free gift to you ..... Gregg Butler
