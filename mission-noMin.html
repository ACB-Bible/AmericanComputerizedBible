This is My Mission



Revelation 21:5 "Behold I'm making all things new again. And he said, 'Write this: These words are true and faithful'. "

My mission is to reach out and tell the whole world about Jesus Christ because He said, "Go out into all the world, and preach the gospel to every creature. Anyone that believes and is baptized will be saved, but whoever does not believe will be condemned." Mark 16:15-18.

The American Computerized Bible is a a very small part of God's ministry, and it's a mission that He's given to me to fulfill. The Twenty-First Century Bible is a new, twenty-first century, modern version of the Bible that's being translated from various other versions of the ancient biblical texts, and it's another part of this ministry. I hope you find this work to be a high quality and useful online Bible for your Biblical studies, and I hope it helps you with your mission to preach God's word to the entire world.

I believe if anyone at all anywhere finds salvation through these efforts, then the mission God has given me will have been accomplished.

God loves you, I love you, and this is God's free gift to you ..... Gregg Butler
