The 'American Computerized Bible' is a ministry the Lord has laid on my heart and it's a work in progress and it's actually two projects in one, the first part of the project is the technological part which is the actual web page and other tools being coded for editing the Bible. It will also contain many different public domain versions of the Bible, including The 'Twenty First Century Bible' which is a new version of the Bible that I'm translating from various ancient texts and 'The King James Version' of the Bible which was first published in 1611. The 'Twenty First Century Bible' is a living Biblical translation and it's inspired by the teachings of Jesus for people in the twenty-first century and beyond to study. As of February 25, 2023, there are eight public domain versions in the ACB-Bible database, and except for editing and proof reading, translation through the book of Leviticus Chapter: 11, is mostly complete, until tomorrow. It's my hope that a computerizd Bible will help you and others to see and hear the good news of salvation that Jesus asked us to tell the world about...Gregg Butler
