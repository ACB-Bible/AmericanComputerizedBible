Twenty-First Century Version

Genesis 1

1 In the beginning, God created Heaven and Earth. 2 The Earth was void and without form, darkness covered the face of the deep, and God's Spirit moved over the waters. 3 And God said, "Let there be light," and there was light. 4 God saw that the light was good, and he separated the light from the darkness. 5 God called the light Day and the darkness Night. That morning and that evening were the first day.

6 And God said, "Let there be a firmament in the middle of the waters, and it'll divide the waters from the waters." 7 God made the firmament and divided the waters under the firmament from the waters above the firmament, and it was so. 8 God called the firmament Heaven. That morning and that evening were the second day.

9 And God said, "Let the waters under Heaven be gathered together into one place and let the dry land appear," and it was so. 10 God named the dry land Earth and He called the waters the seas, and God saw that it was good. 11 And God said, "Let the Earth bring forth every kind of grass, seed-bearing herb, and fruit-bearing tree, each with their seed," and it was so. 12 And the Earth brought forth every kind of grass, herb, and fruit-bearing tree with their seed, and God saw that it was good. 13 That morning and that evening were the third day.

14 And God said, "Let there be lights in the Heavens to divide the day from the night and let them be a sign for the seasons, days, months, and years. 15 Let the lights in the Heavens give light on the Earth," and it was so. 16 God made two great lights, the Sun to rule the day, and the Moon and Stars to rule the night. 17 God placed the lights in the Heavens to give light to the Earth. 18 And to divide the light from the darkness and to rule over the days and nights, and God saw that it was good. 19 That morning and that evening were the fourth day.

20 And God said, "Let the waters bring forth abundant life with living creatures that move and birds that fly in the Heavens above the Earth." 21 God created an abundance of great whales and every kind of living moving creature that lived in the waters, plus every type of winged bird, and God saw that it was good. 22 And God blessed them and said, "Be fruitful, multiply, and fill the waters of the seas with abundant life, and let the birds multiply abundantly upon the Earth." 23 That morning and that evening were the fifth day.

24 And God said, "Let the Earth bring forth every kind of living creature including every kind of cattle, every kind of animal, everything that creeps, and every other living thing on Earth" and it was so. 25 God created animals of every kind. He created every kind of cattle, everything that creeps, and every other living thing on Earth, and God saw that it was good.

26 And God said, "Let us make man in our image, a man who looks like us. And he'll preserve and control all the fish, birds, cattle, everything that creeps, and every other living thing on Earth." 27 So God created man in his image, both male and female. 28 God blessed them and said, "Be fruitful, multiply, and replenish the Earth. Take control of the fish, birds, and every other living thing on Earth."

29 And God said, "Behold, I've given you every seed-bearing herb and every seed-bearing fruit tree on Earth for you to eat. 30 I've given you every living thing on Earth, every beast, every bird, every green herb for food, everything that creeps, and every other living thing on the Earth," and it was so. 31 God saw that everything he'd created was very good. That morning and that evening were the sixth day.
