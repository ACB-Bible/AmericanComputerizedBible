The "American Computerized Bible"® is only one part of the acbbible.com® ministry, and it's a work in progress. It's a mission of love, and it currently contains eight different versions of the Holy Bible. The "American Computerized Bible"® website itself consists of the actual computer source code that makes the website work. This work has consisted of many years of researching and finding usable Bible text files that could be programmatically transferred into a workable database that can be read and used by the website. Most of these files were scavenged from various online sources, and custom computer programs have been written to transfer each one of them into a useable database format.

The "Twenty-First Century Bible"® is another part of this ministry ministry, and it's included in the "American Computerized Bible"® that I'm translating it from various versions of the ancient texts, including the "King James Version" which was first published in 1611. The "Twenty-First Century Bible"® is a living Biblical translation and it's inspired by the teachings of Jesus Christ. It's a gift from God for people of the twenty-first century and beyond to study. As of April 26, 2023, except for editing and proof reading, translation through the book of Leviticus Chapter: 14 is mostly complete, that is until tomorrow.

God loves you, we love you, and this is God's free gift from us to you.
